Fruits of the Rainbow!

Name: Ethan Mayer, Cal Hokanson-Fuchs NetID: etmayer@iastate.edu, calhf@iastate.edu Date: September 23rd 2023 Professor: Abraham Aldaco

Apples are crisp and refreshing fruits known for their sweet and slightly tart flavor. They come in various varieties, each with its unique taste and texture. Apples are a good source of dietary fiber and essential nutrients, making them a popular and versatile snack enjoyed worldwide.

Yum!

Bananas are creamy, yellow fruits with a naturally sweet taste. They are a quick and convenient snack, packed with potassium and vitamins. Bananas are not only delicious on their own but also a versatile ingredient in smoothies, desserts, and baking.

Yum!

Blueberries are small, round, and deep blue-purple in color. They are renowned for their sweet and slightly tangy taste. Blueberries are packed with antioxidants and are frequently used in breakfast dishes like pancakes, muffins, and yogurt parfaits, as well as enjoyed fresh.

Yum!

Grapes are bite-sized fruits that come in various colors, including green (Thompson seedless), red (Concord), and black (Concord). They are sweet, juicy, and often enjoyed in clusters. Grapes can be eaten fresh or used to make juice, wine, and raisins, offering a delightful burst of flavor with each bite.

Yum!

Oranges are juicy citrus fruits with a vibrant orange color. They are celebrated for their zesty, tangy flavor and are a rich source of vitamin C. Oranges can be enjoyed as a fresh, thirst-quenching snack or squeezed into refreshing orange juice.

Yum!

Kiwi, often referred to as the "fuzzy fruit," is a small, green fruit with tiny black seeds and a bright green flesh. Kiwis offer a unique combination of sweet and slightly tart flavors and are renowned for their high vitamin C content. They are typically eaten by cutting the fruit in half and scooping out the flesh with a spoon.

Yum!
